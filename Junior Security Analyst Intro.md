### - SOC (Security Operations Center) is a team that protects a company from cyber attacks
### - You don’t work alone — many people support you in different roles
### - SOC Engineers set up and manage security tools
### - Senior Analysts help investigate complex and serious attacks
### - SOC Manager makes sure everything runs smoothly
### - All team members work together to detect, analyze, and stop threats

## Task 3: A Day in the Life of a Security Analyst
<img width="1140" height="801" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fccfbbc7-a524-4b1a-bf68-211ab4e5090c" />

### Being a Security Analyst
### - A Security Analyst works on the front line to defend systems from cyber attacks
### - The job requires continuous learning because new threats appear every day
### - During a typical work shift:
### - You handle many "tickets" (alerts or issues to investigate)
### - Each ticket represents a possible security problem
### - You must analyze and resolve them quickly
### - Rewarding part of the job:
### - Stopping real cyber attacks
### - Protecting the organization from damage
### - Understanding how real-world attacks happen
